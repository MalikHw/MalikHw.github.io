Video Screensaver

play any video as your android screensaver. made this cuz i couldn't find it on the play store. also i was bored.

download apk

how to use

  1. open the app
  2. hit Choose Video and pick any mp4 — or tap Browse Catalog for something pre-made
  3. tweak playback stuff (sound, loop, scale mode)
  4. hit Save
  5. tap Open System Daydream Settings and set this as your screensaver
  6. plug in your phone and watch it go

scale modes

fitwhole video visible, black bars if needed
stretchfills screen, ratio goes bye bye
zoomfills screen, crops the edges — default, looks the best imo
adaptfit + explicit black bars, great for screwed videos on wide screens
