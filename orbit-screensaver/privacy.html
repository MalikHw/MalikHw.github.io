Orbit Screensaver

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Overview

Orbit Screensaver ("the app/Software") is a free Android/Windows screensaver made by MalikHw47. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data Collection

The app itself does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Used on Android 10–12 only, to let you pick a background or cube image from your gallery. The image stays on your device.

Third-Party Services – Google Play Billing

The Play Store version of the app includes an optional donation feature powered by Google Play Billing. If you choose to make a donation, the payment is processed entirely by Google. The app does not receive, store, or transmit any payment or financial information. Google's handling of payment data is governed by the Google Payments Privacy Notice.

Images You Choose

If you pick a custom background or cube image, that image is read locally on your device and used only for display within the screensaver. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
