Orbit Screensaver

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

Orbit Screensaver ("the app") is a free Android screensaver made by MalikHw47. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Used on Android 10–12 only, to let you pick a background or cube image from your gallery. The image stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Images You Choose

If you pick a custom background or cube image, that image is read locally on your device and used only for display within the screensaver. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Children

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
